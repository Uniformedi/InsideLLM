InsideLLM — Commercial Model

Open core: the platform is free to run, paid to scale. BSL 1.1 today, Apache 2.0 in 2030. No per-seat tax on Community; predictable per-instance pricing for Professional; fleet-wide contracting for Enterprise.

Effective 2026-05-12 (draft) Cataloged 2026-04-22 License BSL 1.1 → Apache 2.0 on 2030-04-11

Principle: the whole platform works without a license. All core capabilities — DLP, OPA policy, hash-chained audit, RAG, Governance Hub, industry bundles — ship under BSL 1.1 and are free to self-host for single-instance use. Paid tiers unlock multi-instance fleet operation, support SLAs, and managed-deployment options. Custom-fit pricing for Parent Organization-scale portfolios.

FAQ

Can I really self-host the whole thing for free?

Yes. BSL 1.1 allows self-hosted, single-instance, non-competitive use at no cost. All features are there. Pay when you need multi-instance fleet operation, support SLAs, managed deployment, or a licensing addendum for procurement.

What does BSL 1.1 actually restrict?

BSL 1.1 permits all use except offering a competing commercial InsideLLM-as-a-service to third parties. Internal use at any scale is fine. The license converts to permissive Apache 2.0 on 2030-04-11.

Our procurement team will not accept BSL. What do we do?

Professional and Enterprise tiers include a licensing addendum that gives you OSI-equivalent terms for your contract period. BSL is the upstream license; your commercial agreement governs your actual use.

We run on Linux, not Windows. Is Hyper-V required?

No. A Linux-native deployment option is available; Terraform paths for KVM / libvirt and vanilla VMware are supported. Contact sales to discuss your specific environment — it is not on the public download page today.

What about token costs to Anthropic / OpenAI?

Your API costs are separate and paid directly to the model provider. InsideLLM enforces per-user, per-team, and per-agent budgets; you control the spend. For cost-sensitive use cases, Ollama on local GPU eliminates per-token cost entirely.

When does pricing "kick in" — at install, first user, first paid tenant?

At the start of your first paid subscription period. Community use is time-unlimited and user-unlimited. Paid tiers bill monthly in advance.

Do you offer non-profit / academic pricing?

Yes. Degree-granting institutions and 501(c)(3) organizations receive 50% off Professional tier. Contact sales.

Can we pilot Enterprise before committing?

90-day Enterprise pilot available with 50% credit against year-1 contract if converted.

What happens to our data if we stop paying?

Your deployment keeps running. The BSL license is perpetual for code you have received. You lose support and managed upgrades. Your data never touches Uniformedi infrastructure under any tier unless you specifically opt into managed-deployment.
