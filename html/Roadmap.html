Cross-release themes

Certification ladder

SOC 2 Type I (Q3 2026) → SOC 2 Type II (Q1 2027) → HITRUST CSF (2027) → ISO/IEC 27001 (2027) → FedRAMP Moderate (2028). Unlocks enterprise + public-sector + healthcare procurement.

Ecosystem alignment

AAIF membership + Secular as sibling FOSS + SAIVAS optional standalone product. Positioning: governed AI with governed context, AAIF-aligned, not competitive with the standard.

Market expansion

US regulated industries (collections + healthcare + financial) → EU AI Act high-risk (2026-2027) → APAC + other common-law jurisdictions. Internationalization is v3.4 gated by multi-region deployment.

Channel maturation

2026: direct sales only. Q3 2026: one MSP channel partner. 2027: 3-5 channel partners across regulated verticals. Hyperscaler marketplace listings add passive lead flow.

IP commercialization

SAIVAS / Humility currently embedded. Q4 2026 decision: commercialize separately as standalone AI alignment policy product, or keep integrated. Decision depends on inbound licensing interest from other AI platforms.

Operational maturity

Bus-factor-of-1 is the headline risk. Target: second senior engineer hired by end of Q3 2026. Compliance or security background preferred.

Roadmap caveats

This document is aspirational, not committed. It will shift based on the 2026-05-12 Parent Organization demo outcome (a conversion unblocks Q3 customer work; a non-conversion redirects Q3 to additional design-partner engagement), Uniformedi's funding runway (direct sales vs. investor-funded path implies different hiring + certification paces), and regulatory environment shifts (the EU AI Act phase-in schedule in particular can pull v3.4 items forward if enforcement begins faster than expected).

No commitment to any release date is implied. Customer commercial contracts govern in case of conflict. This roadmap is an internal strategy document plus a directional signal to design partners.
