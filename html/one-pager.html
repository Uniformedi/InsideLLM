What it is

A self-hosted, on-premises AI governance gateway. One platform serves as both the platform for one regulated operation and the reference tenant for a portfolio of them. The wedge is not capability — it is integrity you can audit. Every LLM interaction inherits DLP scanning, Humility alignment, OPA policy enforcement, per-user budgets, and a hash-chained SHA-256 audit trail. Deployed by a single terraform apply on Hyper-V or a single PowerShell script on WSL2. No cloud dependency.

What Parent Portfolio gets

  • One platform, many deployments. Each portfolio company becomes a tenant with the industry pack appropriate to its vertical. No new product per company.
  • Cross-portfolio governance visibility. Compliance score, at-risk flags, and industry distribution roll up to a single dashboard. Every new company adds a row, not a new integration.
  • Tamper-evident compliance evidence. When a regulator asks "show me everything AI did on this consumer in 2026," it's one query per tenant. The hash chain makes tampering detectable.
  • FOSS-first. Every guardrail that shipped in v3.1.0 can be stood up with open-source components. Paid vendors plug in as optional downstream adapters if needed later.

Honest caveats

  • InsideLLM is a substrate, not a finished compliance program. It reduces the labor of building and auditing AI operations in regulated contexts. It does not replace operator judgment or compliance counsel.
  • FOSS eliminates license cost, not compliance effort. Validation evidence, audit preparation, and SME time survive the tool choice.
  • Every Industry Pack is a defensible starting point, not a finished program. Customers tailor to their specific regulatory posture.
